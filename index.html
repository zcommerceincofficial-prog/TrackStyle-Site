Paint Protection Film (PPF) Phoenix

Our paint protection film installation uses self-healing urethane film to protect your vehicle's most vulnerable panels from rock chips, scratches, and road debris. Coverage options range from partial front-end protection to full vehicle wraps.

Ceramic Coating Phoenix

TrackStyle's ceramic coating service bonds a semi-permanent hydrophobic layer to your paint, enhancing gloss and making maintenance washes faster and easier. We offer entry-level through premium multi-layer packages.

Window Tinting Phoenix

Professional window tinting with ceramic, carbon, hybrid, and dyed film options. Blocks up to 99% of UV rays and significantly reduces interior heat — critical in the Phoenix climate.

Color Change Wraps Phoenix

Over 200 vinyl colors and finishes available for color change wraps including matte, gloss, satin, chrome, and color-shift options. Fully reversible and resale-safe.

Powder Coating Phoenix

Professional powder coating for wheels, brake calipers, suspension components, and engine bay parts. Two to three times more durable than conventional paint.
