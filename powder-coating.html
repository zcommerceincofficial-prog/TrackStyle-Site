What Is Powder Coating?

Powder coating is an electrostatically applied dry finishing process where a powder is sprayed onto a metal surface and then cured under heat to form a hard, durable coating. It produces a consistent, even finish that resists chipping, corrosion, and abrasion far better than spray paint.

Wheel Powder Coating Phoenix
