Why Choose a Wrap Over Paint

A color change wrap is fully reversible. Factory paint stays protected underneath the vinyl, and when you're ready to sell or change the look, the wrap peels off cleanly. This preserves the original paint condition and the vehicle's resale value. A quality wrap also costs significantly less than a professional full respray.

Wrap and Protect — PPF Under Wrap

Some builds call for paint protection film under the wrap on high-impact zones, or ceramic coating over the wrap for added protection and easier maintenance. We can advise on the right combination for your vehicle and use case.
