Custom Vehicle Wraps in Phoenix, AZ — Chrome Delete, Blackout, Livery, and Graphics

TrackStyle offers custom vehicle wraps in Phoenix, Arizona including chrome deletes, blackout wraps, custom liveries, graphic installs, and full or partial color change wraps. All applications are paint-safe and fully reversible.

Chrome Delete and Blackout Wraps Phoenix

Chrome delete wraps cover window trim, grilles, roof rails, and badges in matte or gloss black vinyl for a cleaner, modernized look. A popular upgrade for vehicles with factory chrome accents that the owner wants to black out or match to a darker paint color.

Custom Livery and Graphics Phoenix

TrackStyle designs and installs custom liveries, racing stripes, number plate graphics, sponsor decals, and windshield banners. We handle design, print, and cut in-house for precise results. Pair with a color change wrap for a complete custom build finish.

Reversible Color Change Wraps

Full vehicle or partial accent wraps in gloss, satin, matte, or metallic finishes. Factory paint is protected underneath the vinyl. The wrap can be removed cleanly when you're ready to change the look or sell the vehicle. See our dedicated color change wraps page for the full finish selection.

The Custom Wrap Process at TrackStyle

Every custom wrap project starts with a consultation where we discuss your vision and confirm materials. We then schedule the installation appointment, prep the vehicle properly, and execute the install with tight seams and smart panel placement. Get in touch to start your custom wrap project.

Paint-Safe and Reversible

Premium vinyl wraps protect your factory paint during the wrap's lifespan. When the wrap is removed by a professional, the original paint underneath is preserved. No permanent commitment required. Also consider paint protection film installed under your wrap for an extra layer of protection.
