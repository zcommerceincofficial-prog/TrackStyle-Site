What Is Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to a vehicle's exterior that chemically bonds to the factory paint. Unlike wax or sealants, ceramic coating does not wash away. It creates a hard, sacrificial layer that protects the paint beneath from UV rays, bird droppings, road chemicals, and environmental contamination.

Hydrophobic Protection in Phoenix Heat

Phoenix's sun and heat are harsh on automotive paint. Ceramic coating provides UV protection that slows paint fading significantly. The hydrophobic surface repels water, preventing water spots from forming after rain or washing — a major benefit in Arizona's hard water environment.

Ceramic Coating Gloss Enhancement

A properly applied ceramic coating deepens paint gloss and enhances color vibrancy. Many customers report their paint looks better a year after coating than it did at the dealership.
